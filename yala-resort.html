Green Villa Yala Holiday Resort

Wildlife Adventure at Sri Lanka's Premier National Park

Recognized "Bird Friendly Resort"

Your Gateway to Yala National Park

Green Villa Yala is a lakefront villa located at Weweyaya, Yodakandiya in Tissamaharama, offering the perfect base for your wildlife adventure. Just 30 minutes to Yala National Park, we provide comfortable accommodation and professional safari tour arrangements.

We can arrange jeeps for your safari visit to the nearby National Park and to other nature sanctuaries close to our villa, as well as transport to tour anywhere within the region.
