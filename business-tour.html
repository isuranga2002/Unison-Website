Business Tour in Sri Lanka

7-Day Comprehensive Business and Investment Tour

Explore Business Opportunities

Join our exclusive 7-day Business Tour designed for foreign investors and entrepreneurs looking to explore business opportunities in Sri Lanka. Experience the country's diverse business landscape while visiting key commercial hubs and cultural attractions.

Our carefully curated tour takes you through Negombo, Kandy, Bentota, and Colombo, offering unique insights into investment opportunities, networking with local businesses, and understanding the Sri Lankan market firsthand.

7-Day Tour Itinerary

Negombo → Kandy → Bentota → Colombo

1-2

Negombo

Start your journey in Negombo, a coastal city near the international airport. Explore the fishing industry, visit local businesses, and understand coastal trade opportunities.

  • City tour and orientation
  • Local business visits
  • Fishing industry insights
  • Coastal trade opportunities
3-4

Kandy

Visit Kandy, Sri Lanka's cultural capital and hill country commercial hub. Explore tea plantations, spice gardens, and highland business opportunities.

  • Tea plantation tours
  • Spice garden visits
  • Cultural heritage sites
  • Highland business meetings
5

Bentota

Experience Bentota's thriving tourism and hospitality sector. Explore hotel industry, water sports business, and coastal real estate opportunities.

  • Tourism industry insights
  • Hotel and resort tours
  • Real estate opportunities
  • Water sports businesses
6-7

Colombo

Conclude your tour in Colombo, the commercial capital. Visit the business district, meet potential partners, explore investment opportunities, and finalize business connections.

  • Business district tour
  • Networking events
  • Investment seminars
  • Business partner meetings
