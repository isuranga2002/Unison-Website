The Island of Gems

Sri Lanka, known as the "Ratna-Dweepa" (Island of Gems), has been renowned for centuries as one of the world's premier sources of precious gemstones. Our beautiful island produces some of the finest sapphires, rubies, and other precious stones sought after by collectors and jewelry makers worldwide.

Unison provides exclusive access to authentic Sri Lankan gemstones through established wholesale markets in Ratnapura and Beruwala. We guide foreign buyers through the entire process, from selection to certification, ensuring quality, authenticity, and fair pricing.

Wholesale Market Access

Ratnapura

Known as the "City of Gems," Ratnapura is Sri Lanka's gem mining capital. This historic gem market offers direct access to miners and dealers, providing authentic stones at competitive wholesale prices.

  • Direct from source
  • Wide variety of stones
  • Competitive wholesale rates
  • Mining area visits

Beruwala

Beruwala's coastal gem market is a major trading hub where dealers from across Sri Lanka converge. This established market offers excellent opportunities for bulk purchases and export-quality stones.

  • Major trading center
  • Export-quality stones
  • Bulk purchasing options
  • Convenient coastal location
